MOTTO

With this motto of NSS "Not Me But You", the program aims to instill the idea of social welfare in students and inspire them to provide service to society without bias. NSS volunteers work to ensure that everyone who is needy gets help to enhance their standard of living and lead a life of dignity.


MISSION

To provide a nurturing ground for holistic and integral development of future citizens who are persons of 5C's - Conscience, Character, Compassion, Commitment and Competence.


VISION

Spirit lead ambassadors for a better world of equality, harmony and excellence.
